What about me?

I try to use design as a framework to take ideas, and I genuinely believe that learning through researching and prototyping can breed innovation. I try to use conceptualisation techniques to generate ideas that allow me to evolve these concepts through testing and constant iteration. I graduated from the National College of Art and Design, Dublin, with a BA in Interaction Design and am currently finishing my MSc in Interactive Digital Media at Trinity College Dublin. My mission lies in exploring interactions within 2D and 3D digital environments, where I hope to use my range of multidisciplinary skills to improve any experience.
